# Artory — Product Overview

Artory is a creative community platform connecting artists (guides) with learners. It supports artwork sharing, course/workshop enrollment, social interaction, and art purchases.

## Core User Roles

- **learner** — browses artworks, enrolls in courses, uploads practice works, purchases art
- **guide** — creates and sells artworks, hosts courses, mentors learners
- **admin** — manages platform content, users, and subscriptions

## Key Features

- Artwork gallery with likes, comments, and purchasing
- Course/workshop creation and enrollment with applicant profiles
- Practice work uploads by learners (optionally linked to a guide)
- Subscription plans (Free, Trial, Premium) gating feature access
- Stripe integration for artwork purchases and subscription billing
- Google OAuth + email/password authentication
- Notifications for enrollment requests, likes, and comments
- Global search across artworks, courses, and guides
